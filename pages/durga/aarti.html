Devanagari Script

जय अम्बे गौरी, मैया जय श्यामा गौरी ।
तुमको निशदिन ध्यावत, हरि ब्रह्मा शिवरी ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥1॥

मांग सिंदूर विराजत, टीको मृगमद को ।
उज्जवल से दो‌उ नैना, चन्द्रबदन नीको ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥2॥

कनक समान कलेवर, रक्ताम्बर राजै ।
रक्त पुष्प गलमाला, कण्ठन पर साजै ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥3॥

केहरि वाहन राजत, खड़ग खप्परधारी ।
सुर नर मुनिजन सेवक, तिनके दुखहारी ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥4॥

कानन कुण्डल शोभित, नासाग्रे मोती ।
कोटिक चन्द्र दिवाकर, राजत सम ज्योति ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥5॥

शुम्भ निशुम्भ विडारे, महिषासुर घाती ।
धूम्र विलोचन नैना, निशदिन मदमाती ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥6॥

चण्ड मुण्ड संघारे, शोणित बीज हरे ।
मधुकैटभ दो‌उ मारे, सुर भयहीन करे ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥7॥

ब्रहमाणी रुद्राणी तुम कमला रानी ।
आगम निगम बखानी, तुम शिव पटरानी ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥8॥

चौसठ योगिनी गावत, नृत्य करत भैरुं ।
बाजत ताल मृदंगा, अरु बाजत डमरु ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥9॥

तुम हो जग की माता, तुम ही हो भर्ता ।
भक्‍तन् की दुःख हरता, सुख-सम्पत्ति करता ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥10॥

भुजा चार अति शोभित, खड़ग खप्परधारी ।
मनवांछित फल पावत, सेवत नर नारी ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥11॥

कंचन थाल विराजत, अगर कपूर बाती ।
श्री मालकेतु में राजत, कोटि रतन ज्योति ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥12॥

श्री अम्बे जी की आरती, जो को‌ई नर गावै ।
कहत शिवानन्द स्वामी, सुख सम्पत्ति पावै ।
ॐ जय अम्बे गौरी ॥13॥



Meaning in English

Jai Ambe Gauri Maiya, Jai Shyama Gauri
Tumko Nish-din Dhyavat, Hari Brahma Shivri
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥1॥
Glory to you, divine mother Gauri, glory to you, O Parvati (Shyama), who is so rich in maiden grace, the object of daily meditation by Vishnu, Brahma and Shiva.

Maang Sindoor Virajat, Tiko Mrig-mad Ko
Ujjwal Se Dou Naina, Chandra Badan Niko
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥2॥
On your forehead is a resplendent mark of vermilion along with a mark of musk which signifies signifies good luck. Your twin eyes are bright and your face is as beautiful as the moon.

Kanak Samaan Kalewar, Raktaambar Raaje
Rakt Pushp Galmala, Kanthan Par Saaje
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥3॥
Your body with a tinge of gold is splendidly dressed in red attire; on your throat lies a wreath of red blossoms just as beautiful as a necklace.

Kehri Vahan Rajat, Kharag Khapar Dhaari
Sur Nar Muni Jan Sevat, Tinkay Dukh Haari
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥4॥
Your vehicle, the lion, is in keeping with your splendid form; you bear a sword and a skull in your hands, and on you attend the gods, men, hermits and your votaries whose grief you drive away.

Kanan Kundal Shobhit, Naasagre Moti
Kotik Chandra Divakar, Rajat Some Jyoti
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥5॥
You are adorned with rings on your ears and with pearl on the tip of your nose, your radiance looks as splendid as that of myriad of suns and moons.

Shumbh Nishumbh Vidare, Mahishasur Ghati
Dhumra Vilochan Naina, Nishdin Madmati
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥6॥
Slayer of the demon Mahisha, you tore apart the bodies of Shumbha, Nishumbha and Dhuumravilochana. In the battle waged against them your eyes reflected a frenzy of fury everyday and night.

Chandh Mundh Sanghare, Shonit Beej Hare
Madhu Kaitabh Dou Maare, Sur Bhaya-heen Kare ॥7॥
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥7॥
Your vehicle, the lion, is in keeping with your splendid form; you bear a sword and a skull in your hands, and on you attend the gods, men, hermits and your votaries whose grief you drive away.

Brahmani Rudrani, Tum Kamla Rani
Aagam Nigam Bakhani, Tum Shiv Patrani
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥8॥
You are the beloved consort of Brahma, Rudra and Vishnu. The Vedas and the Shastras describe you as the queen consort of Lord Shiva.

Chousath Yogini Gavat, Nritya Karat Bhairon
Baajat Taal Mridanga, Aru Baajat Damaroo
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥9॥
Sixty-four Yoginis chorus your praise and glorify you, while Shiva dances in tune to the accompaniment of the sound of Mridanga and drum.

Tum Ho Jag Ki Maata, Tum Hi Ho Bharta
Bhaktan Ki Dukh Harata, Sukh Sampati Karata
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥10॥
You are mother of the entire universe, its sustainer, reliever of your devotees’ affliction and bestower of happiness and prosperity.

Bhuja Chaar Ati Shobit, Khadag Khappar-dhari
Man-vaanchit Phal Pavat, Sevat Nar Naari
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥11॥
The four arms have adorned your persona, while the hand raised in benediction reveals your benign aspect. Those men and women who worship shall have all their cherished wishes ever fulfilled.

Kanchan Thaal Virajat, Agar Kapoor Baati
Shri Maal-ketu Mein Rajat, Koti Ratan Jyoti
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥12॥
In a golden platter are beautifully laid aloe and camphor, both of which have lighted to be waved before you; in the radiance of your forehead is reflected the splendour of the myriad gems.

Shri Ambe-ji Ki Aarti, Jo Koi Nar Gaave
Kahat Shivanand Swami, Sukh Sampati Paave
Om Jai Ambe Gauri ॥13॥
Anyone who does your pooja, says Shivanand Swami that they will always get health and happiness in their lives.
